what is a computer technician?

a computer technician is a person who repairs and maintains computers and servers. the technician’s responsibilities may extend to include building or configuring new hardware, installing and updating software packages, and creating and maintaining computer networks. computer repair technicians work in a variety of settings, encompassing both the public and private sectors. because of the relative newness of the profession, institutions offer certificate and degree programs designed to prepare new technicians, but computer repairs are frequently performed by experienced and certified technicians who have little formal training in the field

what steps do i need to follow to become a computer technician?

  • learn about computers
  • gain experience
  • get formal computer training
  • obtain certifications
